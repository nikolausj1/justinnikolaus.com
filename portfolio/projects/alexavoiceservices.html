Alexa Voice Services

Company Amazon
Role Design Lead
Responsibility Scalable design systems, external partner enablement
Year 2018

Overview

Alexa Voice Service brings Alexa to partner devices natively. My team worked with these companies to create the best Alexa integration for their products.

Problem

The Attention System is the lights and sounds that show what Alexa is doing on a device. When I joined the team, I found that partners were trying to copy the Echo's animations by watching them. Others had gone off on their own and created completely different versions.

Sonos Partnership

I was the design owner for Sonos, our top Alexa built-in partner. Sonos wanted a unique identity with custom sound and light patterns. I needed to make the case for consistency: users shouldn't have to learn a different interaction model just for one device.

After presenting user journey scenarios that showed how disjointed their proposed experience would feel, Sonos agreed. We found a solution that gave them the aesthetic control they wanted while staying true to our design principles.

Impact

The Sonos collaboration revealed that our growing partner base needed better self-serve resources. I documented the entire sound and light system, incorporating lessons from Sonos, and published it as an online guide for all partners. Support requests to my team dropped 80%, and internal teams adopted the guide when building new Echo devices.
